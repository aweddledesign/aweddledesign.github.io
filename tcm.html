Brand Manager for Turner Classic Movies Immersive Class

Team Members:
Video: Nicole Lehrman, Dustin Grissom, Henry Tegeler
Writing: Sarah Loyd, Madeline Grosh, Cassandra Grosh

Role: Brand Manager, and Design

I was brand manager for an immersive class at Ball State in Summer 2016. I designed a styleguide, website, merchandise, and graphics for Turner Classic Movies as part of a campaign to promote their Slapstick Fall film festival and the online slapstick course.

I interviewed for and was awarded the oppurtunity to oversee the brand and ensure that all work we put out looked cohesive.

At the end of the class we presented our campaign plan to Turner Classic Movie's General Manager Jennifer Dorian, executive Shannon Clute and other Turner employees.
