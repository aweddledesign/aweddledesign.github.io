Voodoo Vintage

Role:
Design, Branding, Lettering

Voodoo Vintage is an identity system for a fictional vintage clothing store. I researched voodoo symbols and illustration styles and designed a logo that incorporated the angular line and dot style typically found in voodoo symbols. I designed clothing tags, buttons, business cards, and a letterhead for the store.
