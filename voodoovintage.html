Voodoo Vintage

I was asked to design a logo, business cards, and a letterhead for a class Sophomore year for a store called Voodoo Vintage. I designed a typeface reminiscent of voodoo patterns to use for the title treatment. Going beyond the project, I decided to make merchandise for the store, making buttons that recalled symbols from the rest of the materials, and designing a specialty clothing tag that would go on clothes sold at the store.
