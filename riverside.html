Riverside Neighborhood Branding

I worked with residents of Riverside Normal City Neighborhood to help them brand the town, which is known for it's many different styles of houses, a strip of local businesses called the village, and concert venues right off Ball State Universities campus.

I got to know the town when I lived there during college and I loved working with the residents to design a plan for thier neighborhood.

I designed a logo that could appear in print, digitally, and on a wooden sign that welcomed people to the neighborhood.

I also worked as a design consultant, guiding residents of the neighborhood and providing technical skill to help them design and install neighborhood crosswalks.
