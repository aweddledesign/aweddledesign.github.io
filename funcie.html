Funcie

Funcie is a personal project I developed my Sophomore year based on the tongue-in-cheek name locals have for my college town: Muncie, Indiana. “Funcie” playfully jabs Muncie for being a quiet town without much to do or stands for “F*** Muncie” depending on who you ask. I designed a warm, retro logo for Funcie and applied it to t-shirts, patches, and other merchandise which I sold through a local concert venue. The logo was also approved by Snapchat and is a geofilter for Muncie, Indiana.
