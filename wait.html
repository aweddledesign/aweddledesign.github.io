Wait

Role: Branding

“Wait” is a project that I am still developing. The project intends to redesign sex education to be more comprehensive and honest. I chose to redesign sex education because I believe that abstinence-only education prioritizes abstinence over health. The current program encourages abstinence-only knowing that not everyone will choose to wait instead of being realistic about sex so that people can make informed decisions for their own health. The current program also does not discuss consent or other sexual orientations and often leaves students uninformed.
