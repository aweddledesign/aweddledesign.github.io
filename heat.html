Extreme Heat Site

Heat is a website designed by the Digital Corps to promote an architecture class at Ball State. The class sends students to extreme climates to build temporary structures. I contributed to web design, coding, and identity design for this project. We also designed a blog for the class. Check it out here: Extreme Heat Site
