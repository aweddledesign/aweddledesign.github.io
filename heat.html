Heat

Team Members:
Project Manager: Leah Callahan
Designers: Sydney Patton, Grace Winiger, Emily Mixter, Ian Buchanan
Developers: Brandon Groff, Michael Adair
Video: Jacob Guenin, Dustin Grissom
Comm: Miller Kern

Role: Design

Heat is a website designed by a team of students at the Digital Corps for an architecture class at Ball State. The class sends students to extreme environments to build temporary structures to shelter them from the elements. The project consists of identity and web design.
