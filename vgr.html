Village Green Records

Village Green Records (VGR) is a colorful, eclectic record store in Muncie, Indiana. I drew inspiration from the clouds and blue sky painted onto the floor of the store and the motion and shape of a record for my design. I made an interactive, playful logo. It is colorful to incorporate the feeling of the store, with bands that recall the image of a rainbow.
