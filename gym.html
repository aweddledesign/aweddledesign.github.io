Gym App

Ball State Gym App is an app I designed during my training at the Digital Corps. I was told to design an app for something the University needed so I chose a gym app. Currently, gym schedules are displayed via pdfs that are only easily accessed from a computer. I designed an app that would be very accessible, simple and clear with schedules, classes, and hours readily available.
