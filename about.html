My name is Anna Weddle.

I am a freelance designer, illustrator, and photographer currently in Indianapolis. I love travel, art, and reading about art and travel.

For the past few years, I've worked with clients in a very hands-on work environment. The Digital Corps helped me work in a collaborative team with people from many different disciplines such as development and video, helped me establish good design habits and gave me real world experience while in college.

So far, my portfolio includes branding for a band, and a personal project Funcie in which I created a logo for my town and successfully marketed it as merchandise and a Snapchat geofilter, earning me Millenial cred. I have also designed multiple apps and websites as well as countless web and print materials for Ball State University as well as rebranding for various University offices. I've also used personal projects to develop my Photography, Art Direction, and Animation. I've also included in my portfolio some Fine Art work such as Sculpture pieces I made for a Neon class.

I use design to talk about issues that are important to me such as feminism and to solve problems in my community. I want to continue challenging myself, and working to improve my environment through design.

Thank you for checking out my work, I would love to hear from you! I am always accepting freelance and looking for new opportunities.
