Grassroots

Grassroots is identity and print design for a fictional greenhouse and community garden in Muncie, IN. We chose to reinvent an abandoned factory along the Mccullough Trail, cleaning it up and turning it into a greenhouse with spaces for the community to interact in. Grassroots will host events that give participants the opportunity to help create a community garden and beautify the trail. The purpose of these events will be to create a greener community, to teach gardening skills, and to encourage people to plant their own gardens. The project consists of identity design, print, deliverables, and a book.
