Econ in Motion Immersive Class

I was asked to designed a logo and T-shirts for an immersive class at Ball State.

The class was designed so that Telecommunications students from Ball State could make short films based on the principles of the French economist, Bastiat. The T-shirts were given to students and investors at the film premiere in Portland, Indiana. The logo will be reused every year for the immersive class.
