I worked with the Digital Corps to make an educational app used by classrooms across Indiana. We worked with teachers, helping them to design lesson plans. We then made videos, games, articles, and other assets that coordinated with the lesson plans to teach kids about Indiana pioneers. I designed and coded educational games for students to play to help them learn about Indiana pioneers. I also did illustrations for the app. We completed the app in August, 2016.
